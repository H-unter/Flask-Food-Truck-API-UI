Brisbane City Council Food Trucks

This year at the Lord Mayor's Business Awards, a new category of award has been added to recognise the contribution of mobile food trucks to Brisbane’s economy. Brisbane City Council recognises that mobile food trucks activate city spaces and help celebrate Brisbane’s climate and cultural diversity (Brisbane City Council 2018) Criteria and terms for entry 1- Be registered in the official BCC food truck database - see the api here 2- The awards are only recognising food trucks that have a minimum of three (3) competing food trucks in their respective categories. These categories have then limited to the most popular 5 categories. The Awards page shows all eligible trucks for this award.
